import { Steps, Aside, Tabs, TabItem } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

Paper Entry supports two workflows. Pick the one that fits your setup.

<Tabs>
<TabItem label="Workflow A — Direct import">

The manager grades the papers and imports directly. No other teachers involved.

**Before you begin:** you need a quiz with at least one `multichoice` or `truefalse`
question, with "Shuffle answers" off. You need `quiz/paperentry:manage`.

<Steps>

1. Open the quiz → **Paper Entry** tab.

2. *(Optional)* In **Export Settings**, tick any extra profile columns (ID number, class, etc.) and click **Save settings**.

3. Click **Download grading sheet**. Students sit their paper exam as normal. Afterwards, fill in the grading sheet with each student's answers from their paper sheet.

4. Scroll to **Direct Import — Admin Override** (collapsed by default), expand it, choose your filled CSV, and click **Import**.

5. Grades appear in the quiz gradebook immediately.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  Re-importing replaces previous Paper Entry attempts — it won't create duplicates.
</Aside>

</TabItem>
<TabItem label="Workflow B — Multi-grader">

Two or more graders mark independently. A comparison table catches every disagreement before grades are committed.

**Before you begin:** same as Workflow A, plus at least two teachers who will grade independently.

<Steps>

1. Open the quiz → **Paper Entry** tab.

2. In **Export Settings**, choose extra profile columns and click **Save settings**.

3. In the **Graders** card, add each independent grader by name.

4. Students sit their paper exam as normal. Each grader then downloads the grading sheet themselves (via the **Download grading sheet** button on their Paper Entry tab view) and fills in student answers from the collected paper sheets independently.

5. Each grader opens the quiz → Paper Entry tab, uploads their filled CSV via **Submit Your Answers**, and clicks **Submit**.

6. Graders see their own conflict status (colour-coded, without seeing other graders' answers) and re-upload until all cells turn green. As manager you monitor the full comparison table with all graders' answers side-by-side.

7. When every cell is **green** (all graders agree), click **Submit to Gradebook**.

8. Graded quiz attempts are created for all students — grades appear immediately.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  Use [Value Substitutions](../../configuration/value-substitutions/) to let graders enter
  `T`/`F` or `1`/`2`/`3` instead of full answer text.
</Aside>

</TabItem>
</Tabs>
