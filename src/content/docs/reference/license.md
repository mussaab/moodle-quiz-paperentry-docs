## Why GPL v3?

Moodle itself is licensed under the GNU GPL v2 or later. All Moodle plugins
distributed through the [Moodle Plugin Directory](https://moodle.org/plugins/)
are required to use a GPL-compatible license. GPL v3 is the recommended choice for
new plugins because it includes additional protections against patent litigation and
tivoisation.

## Third-party components

Paper Entry does not bundle any third-party JavaScript libraries. It uses only:
